Prosthetics

aesthetic, durable and strong
prosthetic solutions

Swift produces a full range of prosthetic devices from full implant-retained prostheses to a single tooth partial denture, as well as Valplast flexible dentures.

Our prosthetic department offers an array of teeth such as Enigma Life, Natura, Ivoclar Vivodent, Myerson and Acrotone amongst others.

There is a dedicated team for each tier of service, consequently providing a consistent high standard of manufacture in line with the service level requested.

Included in the service is the return of all finished partial dentures on duplicate models at the fit stage. Feedback from our clients has shown this minimises any intervention required by the surgeon, therefore, saving valuable chair time.
